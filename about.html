Vison

In a world where we are constantly caring for others, we believe that you must spend time alone, because, only then you can find peace of mind.
This is our vision, because we find it very important for you to have time for yourself. We want to help you create that, one sip at a time.

How we started

Unbeak started with four young creative individuals who wanted to make the life of young parents easier. When having kids they become your first priority, not you, not your relationship, not your job, but your children. We strive to help you create time for yourself whether you are a parent with young children, an important manager in a big company, or both. We do this through our tea with natural herbs. Our message is very simple: take some time for yourself, make a cup of tea, go and sit somewhere on your own, and enjoy the amazing flavors of Unbeak.

Balance

One of Unbeak’s core values is balance. We know you struggle to create balance in your day-to-day life, and that’s where we come in. With our tea, you can create those little me moments that help you live a more balanced life

Equanimity

One of our other core values is equanimity. This stands for mental stability, calmness, and composure. We believe it’s very important to strive towards obtaining this through drinking our tea, as it helps you relieve stress.

Vulnerability

Unbeak’s last core value is vulnerability. You have to be very vulnerable to really be honest with yourself and realize you are neglecting you own needs due to all the other obligations in your life. Only through vulnerable you can learn to spend some moments focusing on you, one sip at a time
